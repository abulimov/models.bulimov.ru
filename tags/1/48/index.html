[WIP] Bedford MWD, 1/48, Airfix, 2018

Nice little model from Battle of Britain: Ready For Battle set. The whole kit is fantastic - it’s ‘new tool’, so perfect quality and level of details. I’ve decided to start with truck just to get back to painting vehicles and try some new techniques. Model quality It’s perfect. Had zero problems with the kit, you have multiple options like open/closed bonnet, with or without tent, and so forth. And the instructions are just great - everything is clear, all new parts in each step are highlighted with color. ...
